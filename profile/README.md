## 🏢 About ANERS

**ANERS** (AI-Native Engineering & Research Systems) is an independent technology organization focused on building production-grade AI platforms, developer tooling, and cybersecurity research infrastructure.

We operate at the intersection of **artificial intelligence**, **software engineering**, and **ethical security research** — designing systems that are not just functional, but research-ready and developer-first.

> *"We build things that matter. No hype. Just code."*

## 🔐 Ethics & Principles

ANERS is built on a strict **white hat** philosophy.

- All security research is conducted in **authorized, controlled environments only**
- We support **responsible disclosure** and the **bug bounty ecosystem**
- No offensive tooling is deployed against systems without explicit permission
- We believe knowledge should be shared — not gatekept
