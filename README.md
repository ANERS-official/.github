## 🔶 What is ANERS?

**ANERS** (AI-Native Engineering & Research Systems) is an independent tech organization founded by [@rixz](https://github.com/rixz-dev) focused on building AI-powered tools, security research platforms, and developer infrastructure — entirely from the ground up.

## 🎯 Focus Areas

- **AI & LLM Engineering** — building and fine-tuning AI platforms with real-world utility
- **Cybersecurity Research** — ethical hacking, vulnerability research, bug bounty
- **Developer Tooling** — IDE, API infrastructure, CLI tools

---

## 🔐 Ethics & Principles

ANERS operates strictly under a **white hat** philosophy.

- All security research is conducted in controlled, authorized environments
- No offensive tooling is deployed against systems without explicit permission
- We believe in responsible disclosure and the bug bounty ecosystem
- Community knowledge-sharing over gatekeeping
